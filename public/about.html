About 2Hype

CS2 Community Servers · India

THE JOURNEY

2Hype started as a small group of friends who were tired of the same experience laggy community servers, no stats to track, and nowhere to improve. We wanted deathmatch servers that actually felt smooth, with configs that matched how top players warm up. So we built them ourselves.

What began as a private setup among a few players grew into a full community. Word spread through scrims, Discord servers, and Steam groups. Players kept asking for more leaderboards, VIP slots, custom modes. One by one, we built it all. 2Hype is that journey from a weekend project to a dedicated CS2 platform built by players, for players.

WHAT WE DO

Multi-Config DM Servers

Low-ping deathmatch with multiple practice configs — pistol only, headshot only, AWP, and mixed. Designed to replicate how pros warm up.

Live Leaderboards

Real-time kill tracking, K/D ratios, headshot percentages, and damage stats. Every session counts. Every kill is on the board.

VIP Membership

Priority server queue, exclusive DM configs, and a VIP badge on the leaderboard. Support the servers, get the perks.

Private & Tournament Servers

Book dedicated servers by the hour or month. Full tournament hosting for teams — custom bracket sizes, live stats, and admin access.

THE MISSION

Our mission is simple: give every Indian CS2 player access to the kind of infrastructure that serious players deserve fast servers, real stats, and a community worth grinding in. No excuses. Just CS.
